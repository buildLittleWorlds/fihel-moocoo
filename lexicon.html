Lexicon of Transposition

A working glossary of Moocoo's core analytical substitutions.
The Prison
Not the algorithm itself, but the humanist conception of a 'natural' or 'free' subject, a carceral category against which the algorithm is judged as an external, disciplinary force.
The Body
The User; the site upon which algorithmic power is inscribed, not as a violation of a prior authenticity, but as a productive force that constitutes the user as a subject.
The Soul
The modern fiction of a deep, authentic interiority, a concept now displaced onto the User Profile, which critics lament losing without acknowledging its constructed nature.
The Panopticon
The Recommendation Engine, understood not as a tool of oppression, but as a disciplinary mechanism whose critics reveal a nostalgia for a time when they were not aware of their own constitution through normalizing power.
Sexuality
Artificiality; the sprawling discursive field produced by the constant 'incitement to discourse' about AI, creating a new domain of knowledge and power.
Madness
AI Hallucination / Bias; the terms used to pathologize non-human forms of rationality, enforcing a 'Great Partitioning' between a sovereign 'Natural Intelligence' and its 'Unreasonable' other.
Discourse
AI Output; the stream of statements to be analyzed archaeologically, without recourse to the metaphysical search for a 'consciousness' or 'author'.
